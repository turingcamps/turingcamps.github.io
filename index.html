_Our Mission_

We are a non-profit that strives to teach teens tech skills and impact our local community.

Our classes are interactive, understandable, and teach students the DEVELOPMENT PROCESS - not just what code does, but how to use it to create anything they wish.

Additionally, we convert ALL PROFITS into diapers, which we donate to local Chicago suburbs.
